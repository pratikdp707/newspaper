Active COVID cases fall below 5,000 in Delhi

NAGPUR

The downward trend in COVID19 cases continued in Delhi with active cases falling below 5,000 on Tuesday, said a health bulletin released by the government. The city reported 316 new cases over 24 hours, taking the total to 14,29,791.
